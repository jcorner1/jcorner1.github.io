About Me

Welcome. I am a researcher focusing on the intersection of meteorology and machine learning. My work leverages deep learning methodologies to better understand extreme precipitation events and the climatology of convective modes.

Research Focus

Currently utilizing ERA5 and CESM2-LE datasets to analyze baroclinic fronts and convective modes. I specialize in applying CNNs and integrated gradients to interpret complex meteorological patterns.
